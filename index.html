About Calgary

We live in Calgary, Alberta and we couldn't think of a better place to bring our family and friends for our wedding than our home. Some of you will already know what Calgary is like in February but for those of you that don't, we will break the news: it's cold. But the Canadian winter, as chilly as it may be, is beautiful, so please remember: there is no bad weather, only bad clothes!

We've recommended our favourite things to eat, see and do in and around Calgary in February. Have a wonderful time!

Eat.

  • Taste
  • Una
  • Borgo
  • Wurst
  • Charcut
  • Vin Room
  • Bistro 2210
  • Anju
  • Bon Terra
  • Rouge

See.

  • Prince's Island Park
  • The Peace Bridge
  • River Walk
  • Stephen Avenue Mall
  • The Calgary Tower
  • Devonian Gardens and The Core
  • Banff National Park: see the Banff townsite, and continue further to the jewel of the Rockies, Lake Louise

Do.

  • Skating at Olympic Plaza
  • Shopping on 17th Avenue and grab some tea where Derek and Hailey first met!
  • Downhill skiing & boarding for beginners in the city at Canada Olympic Park, or for more comfortable carvers at Sunshine Village and Lake Louise Mountain Resort
  • Snowshoeing and cross-country skiing in Fish Creek Provincial Park, or all that and dog sledding in nearby Canmore
